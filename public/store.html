Ordering & Checkout

Do I need an account to buy?+
No! Shoppla supports fully guest checkout. Just add to cart, enter your details, and pay — no sign-up required.
What payment methods do you accept?+
All major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Apple Pay, and PayPal. Payments secured with 256-bit SSL.
What is the order form option?+
Some products offer an order form — you fill in your name, address, quantity and other details. You can then choose to pay on delivery or pay online via a payment gateway.

Shipping & Returns

How long does delivery take?+
Standard free delivery: 3–5 days. Express ($19): 1–2 days. White Glove + assembly ($49): 5–7 days.
What is your return policy?+
30-day hassle-free returns on all items. Contact us, we collect from your home for free, and refund within 3–5 business days.
